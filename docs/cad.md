# STEP is the engineering recipe. GLB is the plated photo.

<ul class="slide-points">
  <li><strong>STEP:</strong> accurate geometry for interchange, measurement, and manufacturing</li>
  <li><strong>GLB:</strong> fast preview for review, web, and Blender</li>
  <li><strong>Good default:</strong> export both, plus PDF drawings and a BOM CSV</li>
</ul>
