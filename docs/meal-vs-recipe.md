# Cooks serve meals. Recipe developers enable meals.

<ul class="slide-points">
  <li>A working rig or instrument is <strong>one good meal</strong>.</li>
  <li>Documentation is <strong>the recipe</strong>.</li>
  <li>The lab needs <strong>the recipe and at least one verified meal</strong>.</li>
</ul>
